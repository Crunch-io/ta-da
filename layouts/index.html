Crunch lets you search, wrangle, and analyze large quantities of data quickly and easily.

A modern platform for analytics

Your data lives in Crunch's high-performance, columnar data store. Instantly search across your datasets, combine them on-the-fly, and analyze them interactively.


Collaborate productively

Deliver data products to your customers — controlling who sees what and what they can do with it. Researchers can work with it safely, creating filters, analyses, complete decks, and fine-grained tab books, without programming or ever having to worry about fragile data file versions or formats. At the same time, data scientists can use the powerful Crunch API to dig deeper using R or Python.


Crunch speaks research

Crunch does things conventional databases and BI tools don’t — with native support for weighting, survey questions and question wording, and missing-data handling from the bottom up.
