* The AI Agent is a smart guide inside Crunch that helps users explore and understand data.
* Reduces the learning curve, helping non-technical users work confidently with data.
* Works alongside users as an assistant, enhancing confidence and speed, not replacing human judgment.

The AI Agent is built to find insights from your dataset, and will not answer questions that cannot be found in your data. If the agent doesn’t know the answer, it will say so. Most importantly, the agent will always cite the variable(s) and filter(s) used to respond, so you are confident in the response.

Clients retain full control over the feature, with the ability to disable the AI Agent at the folder level for any project where the technology is not appropriate or desired.
