Lead Scalability Engineer

Crunch.io is seeking a senior developer to play a leading role in architecting and building our distributed database for interactive data exploration. This is challenging yet fun work on scaling Big Data, API design, and data optimization, operating at the cutting edge of database technologies.

We are a top-notch team of engineers and statisticians who believe that data preparation and analysis should be instant, visual, and collaborative. We have proven experience in delivering fast, compelling insights from large datasets, and we’re building on that experience to create a platform for statistics in the cloud.

This position is a great fit for you if:

  • You have a deep understanding of how databases work under the hood, and how distributed systems scale.
  • You have a passion for data, for building tools to work with data, or for building tools that more generally help people solve problems effortlessly.
  • You thrive on the intensity and energy that can only come from working with talented peers in a startup environment.
  • You’re looking for a full-time position, not a freelance gig.

Requirements:

  • Demonstrated experience in developing large-scale distributed systems
  • Expert-level skills in large-scale computational tasks
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Serious commitment to high development standards, including comprehensive testing, in whatever language you’re working
  • A Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, or related
  • Special talents for Python, NumPy, and language design will make you stand out
  • Experience working on a distributed team is a plus; being located somewhere in the Western Hemisphere with a robust internet connection is also helpful

To apply, send your resume to jobs@crunch.io.
