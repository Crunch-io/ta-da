Introducing Modern Analytics

Crunch makes complex analytics simple. Our modern point-and-click interface enables professionals with any skill level to focus on the analysis of complex data, rather than on the structure of the data or on how to use a programming language or tool. The graph is the analytic. You work on the data, at the speed of thought, thanks to instant results provided by inexpensive cloud-based clustered computing.

Modern BI tools, such as Tableau or GoodData, deliver modern, self-service visualization and analytics of structured business transactional data, but they don't understand complex, multi-structured data, which comes in dozens of formats and resides in non-transactional systems such as customer interaction systems.

Legacy advanced analytic platforms, like SPSS or R, do handle complex, multi-structured data, but they were designed decades ago and are hard to use and inefficient programming languages.

Visual, instant and collaborative, Crunch provides a modern point-and-click environment for visualizing and analyzing complex data. Crunch provides modern analytics.
